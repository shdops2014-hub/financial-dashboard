Privacy Policy

Sarah's Home Design, LLC — Last updated: April 28, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Sarah's Home Design, LLC ("SHD", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with the SHD Financial Dashboard application ("the App"). The App is an internal business tool used solely by authorized SHD personnel.

2. Information We Collect

The App accesses the following data from your connected QuickBooks Online account:

  • Profit and Loss report data (revenue, cost of goods sold, operating expenses, net income)
  • Company name and industry information
  • OAuth access and refresh tokens used to authenticate with QuickBooks

We do not collect personal information about end customers, employees, or third parties from your QuickBooks account.

3. How We Use Your Information

Data accessed through QuickBooks is used exclusively to display financial trend charts and key performance indicators within the App. We do not sell, share, or transmit your financial data to any third parties.

4. Data Storage

OAuth tokens are stored securely in Google Apps Script Script Properties, accessible only to authorized SHD administrators. Financial report data is fetched on demand and is not persisted outside of your browser session.

5. Data Security

All communication between the App and QuickBooks is conducted over HTTPS. OAuth tokens are never exposed to the browser or stored in client-side storage.

6. Retention

OAuth tokens are retained until you disconnect QuickBooks from the App or manually revoke access through your Intuit account settings. No historical financial data is stored by the App.

7. Your Rights

You may disconnect the App from your QuickBooks account at any time by clicking "Disconnect QuickBooks" in the App, or by visiting your Intuit account at accounts.intuit.com and revoking access.

8. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact Sarah's Home Design, LLC at the business address on file.
